About Me

MY PASSION

I'm passionate about sports, especially hiking. I love hiking with my friends whenever I have free time. It's not just a hobby for me, but also a way to stay fit and motivated.

Hiking has taught me many valuable lessons. It has instilled in me a strong work ethic and perseverance as I've faced many challenges on various trails. Through team-based hiking challenges, I've learned how to collaborate and work towards collective success, fostering a supportive environment along the way.

These activities have demonstrated my physical endurance and resilience, especially when covering challenging terrains and long distances. I believe this perseverance translates into all areas of my life, constantly pushing me to strive for more and grow through challenges.
